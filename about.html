so while i was at work the other day, waiting for one of my servers to build, i wondered to myself, how many of ashton kutcher's followers actually click on the links that he tweets, so i went ahead and built a little program using the wonderful twitter and bit.ly API. much to my surprise, i found that on most occassions less than 1% of his followers actually go on to click his links. see for yourself. on the 2/18/2010 i had some free time and i just had to code (very rapidly, mind you) this up and make it generic and available for one and all. go on and try some users with some ridiculous number of followers

now, there are a few caveats. if, for example, 2 users tweet the same URL, as far as I know, bit.ly gives both users the same URL, this could overstate the click count. also, some URLs get retweeted like crazy, once again overstating the click count.
