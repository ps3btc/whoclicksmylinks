so while i was at work the other day, waiting for one of my servers to build, i wondered the following: how many of ashton kutcher's followers actually click on the links that he tweets?. so i went ahead and built a little program to do just that. much to my surprise, i found that on most occassions less than 1% of his followers actually go on to click the links that he tweets. go on, see for yourself. you could also try out some users who have a ridiculous number of followers.

now, there are a few caveats. if, for example, 2 users tweet the same URL, as far as I know, both these users are given the same bit.ly link, which could overstate the click count. also, some URLs get retweeted like crazy, once again overstating the click count.
